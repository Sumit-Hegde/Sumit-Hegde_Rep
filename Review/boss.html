My Favourite Architects

Le Corbusier

Le Corbusier was an architect who was born in Switzerland, but was nationalized French in 1930. He is known as the father of modern architecture because he took it to its maximum expression.

Frank Lloyd Wright

Frank Lloyd Wright was an American architect, who started his jobs at the Adler & Sullivan company (builders of skyscrapers in Chicago). His works are characterized by introducing advanced criterias to the time, much more horizontal.

Richard Meier

Richard Meier was an American architect who, after traveling to Europe and meeting Le Corbusier, would begin to apply his principles and characteristics in many of his works. Meier is also one of the architects who stays loyal to his ideas, using lines, planes and volumes.
